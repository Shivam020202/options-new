Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your journey with us.

Can't find your answer? Contact us →

Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross, Aetna, and Medicare. Please contact our office to verify your specific coverage.

What is the first appointment like?
Your initial consultation is a comprehensive 60-minute evaluation where we discuss your history, current challenges, and goals. It's a no-pressure environment to see if we're a good fit.

Is TMS painful?
Most patients report no pain. You may feel a light tapping sensation on your head. There is no downtime, and you can drive yourself home immediately after treatment.
